Founding Story and Mission

TechUplift Foundation was born from the shared vision of individuals who, despite lacking prior technical knowledge, gained their Information Technology education through sponsored learnership programs. Their journey illuminated a critical gap: while many young people in South Africa hold international IT certifications, they often struggle to secure jobs due to a lack of practical experience and soft skills. Witnessing this disparity firsthand, they were driven to take action.

Recognizing a profound gap between the youth in marginalized communities, public schools, and the rapidly evolving ICT industry, they embarked on a mission to bridge this divide. Witnessing the transformative potential of technology education, they founded TechUplift Foundation with a resolute "why" - to empower children and youth (especially the girl child) in underserved areas with digital education and opportunities previously inaccessible to them.
