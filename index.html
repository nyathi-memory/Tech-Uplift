Founding Story and Mission

TechUplift Foundation was founded by individuals who, despite lacking technical backgrounds, gained IT education through sponsored programs. They observed a gap: despite having international IT certifications, many South African youth struggle to find jobs due to a lack of practical experience and soft skills. Motivated to bridge this divide, they established TechUplift Foundation to empower underserved children and youth, particularly girls, with digital education and opportunities previously unavailable to them.
